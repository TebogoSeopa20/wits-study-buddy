Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, student ID, email, phone number
  • Academic Information: Faculty, course, year of study
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Communication Data: Messages and study group interactions

How We Use Your Information

  • Connect you with compatible study partners
  • Facilitate study group organization and communication
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Ensure platform security and prevent misuse

Data Protection

  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel
  • We regularly update our security measures
  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations

Information Sharing
